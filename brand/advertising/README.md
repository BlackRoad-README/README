# Advertising — 12 Production Assets

Based on JOUR 4251 Psychology of Advertising (UMN, Dr. Claire Segijn).

Each ad has: visual spec, headline, body copy, strategic metadata, psychological trigger.

Examples:
- Ad 03: "Can you explain your tools... or do they just work to you?" (expertise anxiety)
- Ad 10: "Would you live in a house where you're not allowed to see the wiring?" (inherent safety)

Framework: ELM model, compliance principles, personalization paradox, packaging psychology.
