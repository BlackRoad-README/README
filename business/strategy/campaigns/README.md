# Marketing Campaigns

Built on JOUR 4251 Psychology of Advertising (University of Minnesota, Dr. Claire Segijn).

## Persuasion Framework

### ELM — Elaboration Likelihood Model

- **Central route** (developers): "OpenAI-compatible API, 15 models, $0 marginal cost, runs on your hardware"
- **Peripheral route** (general users): "Your AI remembers you" (emotional, identity-based)

### Compliance Principles

- **Automaticity:** Make onboarding frictionless
- **Scripts:** Chat UI feels like ChatGPT (familiar sequence)
- **Recognition heuristic:** Get BlackRoad into AI conversations everywhere
- **Least effort:** Don't make them think

### Personalization Paradox

- Benefits must outweigh privacy costs
- "Your AI remembers your preferences" (good) vs "Your AI knows what you did at 3pm" (creepy)

## Three Audiences

| Audience | Route | Message | Channels |
|----------|-------|---------|----------|
| Developers | Central | "One line to switch from OpenAI" | HN, Reddit, Dev.to |
| Sovereignty Seekers | Central + Peripheral | "Your hardware. Your data." | r/selfhosted, r/privacy |
| Identity Builders | Peripheral | "Your AI remembers you" | Twitter, LinkedIn, Product Hunt |
